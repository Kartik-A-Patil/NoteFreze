## Overview

NoteFreze is a thoughtfully crafted open-source application designed to empower you to quickly capture, organize, and share your notes with ease. Built with simplicity and efficiency in mind, it seamlessly integrates into your workflow, allowing you to focus on what matters most: your ideas.

## Key Features

- **Simple and Intuitive Interface:** Capture and access your notes effortlessly with a user-friendly design.
- **Effortless Organization:** Keep your thoughts structured with tags, categories, and efficient search functionality.
- **Seamless Sharing:** Collaborate and share your ideas effortlessly with others.
- **Markdown Support:** Enjoy rich text formatting and a distraction-free writing experience.
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility:** Access your notes seamlessly across multiple devices.
- **Open-Source Freedom:** Contribute to the project's development and customization.
